# Product Overview

NexusTrade is a comprehensive Forex trading platform designed for the Indonesian market that combines AI-powered strategy generation with automated trading capabilities.

## Core Features

- **AI Strategy Generator**: Uses LLM integration (OpenRouter) to generate trading strategies
- **ML Auto-Trading**: Machine learning models for automated trade execution
- **MT5 Integration**: Direct connection to MetaTrader 5 for live trading
- **Multi-Asset Support**: Forex (EURUSD), Crypto (BTCUSD), and Gold (XAUUSD)
- **Real-time Analytics**: Live trading statistics and performance monitoring
- **Subscription Management**: Tiered access control (free, basic, pro, enterprise)

## Target Users

- Indonesian forex traders
- Retail traders seeking automation
- Users requiring MT5 integration
- Traders interested in AI-generated strategies

## Business Model

Subscription-based SaaS with multiple tiers providing different levels of access to AI features and trading capabilities.
